Introduction

The Divisions of Theology

  • There is more than one way to divide the functions of theology and theology is used to encompass biblical, historical, philosophical, and theological studies:
    • Biblical theologyorganizes the theological teachings in the Bible according to prominent biblical themes.
    • Historical theology studies the development of theology from biblical times to the present.
    • Systematic theology arranges Christian beliefs, values, and practices in an orderly and comprehensive manner.
    • >Philosophical theology interacts with philosophy in establishing theology.
    • Practical theology applies Christian beliefs to the church and the world.
